  Campus Life

       The school offers a broad curriculum that covers all major subjects, including science, mathematics, social studies, languages, and more. The teachers at the school are highly qualified and dedicated to providing the best possible education to their students. The school has excellent sports facilities, including two grounds one incampus and another outside the campus.Every year Sports are conducted on this two grounds.wherin students are provided an opportunity to showcase their talent. The school hosts a range of events and activities throughout the year, such as Annual Day Program, Science Exhibition and many more. These events provide students with opportunities to interact with their peers, showcase their talents, and contribute to the community.

Here's a closer look at some of the highlights of Campus life

 Science Exhibition

     The purpose of Science Exhibition at school is to encourage students to explore scientific principles and concepts, to develop an interest in the subject and to recognize the significant role that science plays in our daily lives.Overall, Science Exhibition is an important event that celebrates the wonders of science and inspires students to explore the world around them with a curious and scientific mind. It provides an excellent opportunity for students to develop their interest and passion for science and to become future leaders in the field

  Annual Day

     The Annual Day celebration is an eagerly awaited event that celebrates the achievements and talents of its students. This day is usually celebrated towards the end of the academic year and is an occasion to showcase the diverse talents of the school's students in the areas of academics, sports, and arts. The purpose of the Annual Day celebration is to provide a platform for students to exhibit their skills and to encourage them to explore their interests and passions

 Sports Day

     Sports are an important aspect of student life, that promotes a healthy and active lifestyle. The school has a dedicated sports department that organizes various sporting events and competitions throughout the year, culminating in an annual sports day celebration. The day is dedicated to sports and physical activities, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their athletic abilities and sportsmanship. The event is attended by students, teachers, and parents, who come together to support and encourage the participants. The events are carefully planned and organized, with the aim of encouraging students to participate, compete, and have fun.
